NCR's Best University

NAAC A++

Manav Rachna International Institute of Research and Studies (MRIIRS) is a private university located in Faridabad, Mohali, and other places in India. It is a National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) accredited A++ grade university. The campus covers 60 acres with buildings for engineering, applied sciences, and business studies, as well as an auditorium and central library. Students live in hostels with a separate facility for international students. The university offers various undergraduate, postgraduate, and PhD courses through its schools. Admissions to all courses are both merit and entrance-based. The university accepts the MRNAT (Manav Rachna National Aptitude Test), Uni-GAUGE-E, and SAT for domestic and international applicants. Manav Rachna has a strong focus on research, with over 643 research publications in national and international journals and conferences between 2006 and 2011. The university has a Training and Placement department that helps its graduates find employment, and the Corporate Resource Centre connects students with employers. Manav Rachna has received several rankings and accolades, including a NIRF ranking of 118th among engineering colleges in 2021 and an Outlook India ICARE ranking of 40th among the top 100 private engineering schools in India in 2021.

Udmey Courses

Coursera

Coursera is good because it offers a wide range of courses and degrees from over 300 top universities and companies, providing access to high-quality education from anywhere in the world. It has a large catalog of courses, with over 3,800 courses, 385 specializations, 17 MasterTrack certificates, and 20 degrees. Additionally, Coursera is affordable, with many courses available for free, and others at a lower cost than traditional university programs. It also offers flexible learning, allowing students to learn at their own pace, and from anywhere, making it ideal for busy learners. Furthermore, Coursera has a strong reputation, with many reputable companies and universities partnering with it, and its certificates and degrees are recognized by employers.
